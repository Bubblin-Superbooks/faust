of illustration. The close of the Soldier's Song (Part I. Scene II.) is:—

"Kühn is das Mühen,
Herrlich der Lohn! Und die Soldaten
Ziehen davon."

Literally:

Bold is the endeavor,
Splendid the pay! And the soldiers
March away.

This Mr. Hayward translates:—

Bold the adventure,
Noble the reward—
And the soldiers
Are off.
