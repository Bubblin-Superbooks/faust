Diesira, dies illa,
Solvet soeclum in favilla! (Sound of the organ.)

EVIL SPIRIT

Wrath takes thee! The trumpet peals! The graves tremble! And thy heart
From ashy rest
To fiery torments
Now again requickened,
Throbs to life!

MARGARET

Would I were forth! I feel as if the organ here
My breath takes from me,
My very heart
Dissolved by the anthem!

CHORUS

Judex ergo cum sedebit,
Quidquid latet, ad parebit,
Nil inultum remanebit.
