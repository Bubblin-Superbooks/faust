(Disappears.)

FAUST (overwhelmed)

Not thee! Whom then?
I, image of the Godhead! Not even like thee!

(A knock).

O Death!—I know it—'tis my Famulus! My fairest luck finds no fruition:
In all the fullness of my vision
The soulless sneak disturbs me thus!

(Enter WAGNER_, in dressing-gown and night-cap, a lamp in
his hand._ FAUST turns impatiently.)

WAGNER

Pardon, I heard your declamation;
'Twas sure an old Greek tragedy you read?
In such an art I crave some preparation,
